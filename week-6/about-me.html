Maryland

Maryland (UK: /ˈmɛərɪlənd/ MAIR-il-ənd, US: /ˈmɛrələnd/ (About this soundlisten) MERR-ə-lənd)[9] is a state in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States, bordering Virginia, West Virginia, and the District of Columbia to its south and west; Pennsylvania to its north; and Delaware to its east. The state's largest city is Baltimore,[10] and its capital is Annapolis. Among its occasional nicknames are Old Line State, the Free State, and the Chesapeake Bay State. It is named after the English queen Henrietta Maria, known in England as Queen Mary, who was the wife of King Charles I.[11][12]
