A Breif Summary Of The Topics

The Ancient Age: The Ancient Age page will primarily summarize the very beginnings of human civilization in Mesopotamia (AKA: the land between two rivers, namely the Tigris and the Euphrates) ending with the rise of the Akkadian Empire, one of the first in human history.

The Bronze Age: The Bronze Age page will summarize the rise of great empires just west of Mesopotamia such as Egypt, the Hitties, and their eventual fall during the great Bronze Age Collapse.

The Classical Age: The Classical Age page will go over what is likely the most famous part of the ancient world, the conquest of Alexander the Great, his empire's fall and the rise of a small kingdom turned republic on the Italian Peninsula: Rome.
